ABOUT THE APP

CHMS application allows community health workers and public health workers to carry out their roles more effectively with better communication between registered medical institutions and members of the community using the chat feature on the app.The app is also informative through its blog feature where users are able to access information that concerns their health.All these activities are done online at the users convienience.Users are also in a position to air out issues around them, and this promotes crisis communication,community collaboration and health quality assessment.
